ESO Rollplay

Welcome to Rollplay!

Rollplay is a character profile and dice rolling supplement for roleplayers in The Elder Scrolls Online. The system closely mimics tabletop play, where a game master controls the action for other players. Join the fun from your computer, tablet, or phone!
