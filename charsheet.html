Building Your Character

Name/Race/Etc.

You must specify a character name to save your character. Race, sex, and supernatural type have an effect on stats, so be sure to select the appropriate values.

Attributes

You should begin by choosing your attribute scores. This is a straight 1-for-1 point buy.

Skills

Skills are added much like attributes, but they increase in cost as you increase in rank. However, the governing attribute of a skill controls how quickly the increase occurs.

Knowledge Skills

Knowledge skills are a special class of skill that always have Intelligence as their governing attribute. Some are more difficult to learn and increase in difficulty more quickly.
