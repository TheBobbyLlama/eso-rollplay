ESO Rollplay - Supernatural Types

Werewolf

Attributes: +1 STR, +1 AGI, +1 END
Skill Bonuses: Perception +3; Survival +2.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Silver

The above stat modifiers apply to the werewolf in human form. When the werewolf transforms, they get the following stat modifiers instead:

Attributes: +3 STR, +1 AGI, +2 SPD, +3 END
Skill Bonuses: Perception +4; Survival +2; Unarmed +3.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Poison, Silver
More info at UESP

Vampire

Attributes: +2 STR, +2 WIL, +2 SPD
Skill Bonuses: Blood Magic & Perception +2; Sneak +1.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Flame, Silver

This is the standard ESO form of vampirism, which is acquired by Noxophilic Sanguivoria.

The above stat modifiers apply to the vampire in their regular form. If they transform into a Blood Scion, they get the following stat modifiers instead:

Attributes: +2 STR, +2 WIL, +2 SPD, +1 END
Skill Bonuses: Blood Magic & Perception +2; Sneak & Unarmed +1.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Flame, Silver
More info at UESP

Vampire (Daggerfall)

Attributes: +2 STR, +2 INT, +2 WIL, +2 SPD
Skill Bonuses: Blood Magic & Perception +2.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Flame, Silver

This type of vampire is a catch-all for the nine bloodlines in The Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall.

The nine bloodlines are as follows:

Anthotis - Garlythi - Haarvenu - Kulari - Lyrezi - Montalion - Selenu - Thrafey - Vraseth -

Vampire (Aundae)

Attributes: +2 STR, +4 WIL, +2 SPD
Skill Bonuses: Blood Magic & Perception +2; Destruction & Mysticism +1.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Flame, Silver

This is a magically gifted strain of vampirism, which can be found in Vvardenfell along with its rival clans Berne and Quarra. Members of this clan are traditionally Altmer.

More info at UESP

Vampire (Berne)

Attributes: +2 STR, +2 WIL, +2 AGI, +2 SPD
Skill Bonuses: Blood Magic & Perception +2; Light Armor & Sneak +1.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Flame, Silver

Those with this strain of vampirism are very agile and sneaky. Like Clan Aundae and Clan Quarra, they are based in Vvardenfell.

More info at UESP

Vampire (Quarra)

Attributes: +4 STR, +2 WIL, +2 SPD
Skill Bonuses: Blood Magic & Perception +2; Heavy Armor & Unarmed +1.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Flame, Silver

This strain of vampirism produces strong and fierce warriors. Like Clan Aundae and Clane Berne, they are based in Vvardenfell.

More info at UESP

Vampire (Cyrodiil)

Attributes: +2 STR, +2 WIL, +2 SPD, +2 PER
Skill Bonuses: Blood Magic & Perception +2; Illusion & Speechcraft +1.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Flame, Silver

This type of vampire is especially adept at passing as a mortal. It is primarily found in Cyrodiil, as featured in The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion.

More info at UESP

Vampire (Volkihar)

Attributes: +2 STR, +2 WIL, +2 SPD
Skill Bonuses: Blood Magic & Perception +2.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Flame, Silver

An ancient and powerful form of vampirism, which is acquired by Sanguinare Vampiris. It is the type of vampirism featured in The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim.

The above stat modifiers apply to the vampire in their regular form. If they transform into a Vampire Lord, they get the following stat modifiers instead:

Attributes: +2 STR, +2 WIL, +4 SPD, +1 END
Skill Bonuses: Blood Magic +4; Destruction, Illusion, Mysticism, Perception & Unarmed +2; Sneak +1.
Resists: Disease
Weak to: Flame, Silver

Volkihar vampires are very powerful and rare. Check with your GM before choosing this vampire type!
