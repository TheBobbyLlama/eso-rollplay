ESO Rollplay - Attributes

Attributes in ESO Rollplay are the same as those used by older installments of The Elder Scrolls series.

When you open the Character Sheet page and begin character creation, you will have 10 attribute points available. Point buy is very simple, 1 for 1. Values selected can range from 2 below the starting value, to 5 above.

Attributes

These are the same attributes that were used by earlier games: Arena, Daggerfall, Morrowind, and Oblivion.

  1. Strength
  2. Intelligence
  3. Willpower
  4. Agility
  5. Speed
  6. Endurance
  7. Personality
  8. Luck

Attributes do not affect skills directly; they affect how quickly skills increase in cost. However, some attributes have additional effects:

  • Intelligence determines how many skill points are available.
  • Agility is used to avoid attacks.
  • Speed determines when your character acts in the turn order.
  • Endurance is used for withstanding damage.
  • Luck gives a chance for dice rerolls - positive or negative, depending on the attribute value.

More info on exactly how many of these attributes are used can be found on the Rolling page.
